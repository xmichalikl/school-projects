Mi A1

Google Pixel in Xiaomi body?

The first Xiaomi smartphone without MIUI

Xiaomi Mi A1 is different from all previous models. Xiaomi picked up the Mi 5X model, rewrote the inscriptions on the back and activated the 800 MHz LTE. But the biggest change is the transition from MIUI to clear Android. We've seen a similar move on Google Play Edition devices - Galaxy S4, HTC One, One M8, and Sony Z Ultra. The cooperation of Xiaomi and Google under the Android One project is an important step towards the availability of this manufacturer's smartphones in global markets.
