Comparison

Xiaomi Mi A1

General parameters

Display size 5,5-inch
Screen resolution 1920×1080
Display ratio 16:9
Operating system Android 9
SD card slot Yes
SD card type micro SDXC
SD card up to 128 GB
Rear camera resolution 12 Mpx
Front camera resolution 5 Mpx
Functions Fast charging, Notification diode, OTG, Fingerprint sensor (rear-mounted)

The Xiaomi Mi A1 is a really good alternative for users who appreciate the current system, timely security updates, and fast and smooth operation of the system itself. Yes, there are a few compromises to consider, such as wider bezels around the display, some missing MIUI enhancements, missing NFCs, or worse photographic results in low light conditions. On the other hand, it is a very well-equipped smartphone, which has virtually no competitor in its price range. Equipment at a given price usually offers less, or less. the devices in the Mi A1 have a much more expensive price tag.

Pros and cons

+ Metal body - Missing NFC
+ Camera - Performance
+ Battery
+ Price

Huawei P30 lite

General parameters

Display size 6,15-inch
Screen resolution 2312×1080
Display ratio 19.5:9
Operating system Android 9
SD card slot Yes
SD card type micro SDXC
SD card up to 512 GB
Rear camera resolution 48+8+2 Mpx
Front camera resolution 24 Mpx
Functions Face unlock, Fast charging, OTG, Fingerprint sensor (rear-mounted)

It is not easy to create a phone that has everything but does not cost a lot of money. Although the Huawei P30 Lite has everything, it doesn't even cost a lot, but it's at the cost of more compromises. For example, the display of this smartphone offers a less popular manufacturing technology and has insufficient brightness value. And the processor cannot deliver as much performance as similarly expensive devices. On the other hand, this does not mean that the Huawei P30 is a bad phone. It also has its advantages, which include a successful construction together with a nice design. It also delights with the main camera, fast charging and large internal storage. Ultimately, therefore, the pros certainly outweigh the negatives.

Pros and cons

+ Construction - System reactions
+ Camera
+ Fast charging
+ Storage
